HUNTING

We have everything the duck, deer, turkey or all around big-game hunter needs. Our waterfowl section is comprehensive and filled with duck calls, waterfowl gear and accessories. From waders to decoys, motion decoys and dog vests, we have everything the duck hunter needs for the waterfowl season.

Outdoor Sportsman also has a great selection of deer and big game hunting equipment. We carry a variety of rifle ammunition, as well as shotgun shells and gun cleaning equipment. We are your one stop shop for all of your hunting needs

FISHING

For the expert of recreational fisherman we have a wide variety of rods, reels, line and bait. The Stockton/San Joaquin Delta is filled with striped bass and sturgeon and we have a huge selection of equipment available to help you catch your limit.

Let our staff help you choose the rod and reel that is right for you. Whether you are salmon fishing in the San Francisco Bay or sitting in your drift boat, the Outdoor sportsman has a wide variety of fishing equipment to meet your needs.
